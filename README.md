Business Sales Performance Analytics
Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Task 1 (2026)

📊 Project Overview
Analyzed 800 business sales transactions across 2023–2024 to uncover revenue trends, top products, regional performance, and profitability insights.

🔑 Key Insights
- December & July are peak revenue months — 40–80% above monthly average
- Office Supplies have a 42% profit margin but only 4.2% revenue share — big opportunity
- Laptop Pro X1 drives 27.4% of all revenue — concentration risk to address

🛠️ Tools Used
- Python (data generation & analysis)
- Chart.js (interactive visualizations)
- HTML/CSS (dashboard)
