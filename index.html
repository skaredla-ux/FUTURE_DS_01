Key Insights
✦ Growth

Q4 Is the Revenue Engine

December 2023 ($62.9K) and July 2024 ($62.9K) represent the two highest-revenue months. Both periods follow promotional cycles and back-to-school / year-end procurement peaks. Aligning stock and campaigns ahead of these windows could amplify performance significantly.

✦ Opportunity

Office Supplies Are Underinvested

Despite a 42% profit margin — nearly double Technology's 20.7% — Office Supplies contribute only 4.2% of total revenue. Increasing SKU range, cross-sell bundling with hardware orders, and targeted promotions could unlock disproportionate profit growth at low incremental cost.

✦ Risk

Revenue Concentration in One Product

Laptop Pro X1 alone accounts for $207K — 27.4% of all revenue. This level of concentration creates vulnerability to supply disruption or competition. Diversifying the technology portfolio and building stronger mid-tier SKUs would reduce this single-product dependency.

Actionable Recommendations
01

Scale Office Supplies Through Bundling

Package Office Supplies with Technology orders. A "starter kit" bundle alongside every Laptop or Monitor purchase can double Office Supplies revenue within 2 quarters while protecting the 42% margin. Consider loyalty subscriptions for repeat consumables.

02

Activate the South Region with Targeted Campaigns

The South region lags the West by $104K in revenue on only a 17% smaller customer base. Localised email campaigns, regionally adjusted pricing, and a dedicated sales rep could close at least half this gap, adding an estimated $50K+ annually.

03

Front-Load Inventory Before Demand Peaks

December and July show revenue spikes of 40–80% above monthly average. Ensuring safety stock of top-5 SKUs 6 weeks before these periods eliminates stockout risk during the highest-value windows. Introduce early-bird discounts in November and June to smooth demand.

04

Reduce Single-Product Revenue Dependency

With Laptop Pro X1 at 27.4% of total revenue, a product discontinuation or margin squeeze would materially impact the business. Invest in growing Monitor 27" and Tablet S8 as secondary anchors. Introduce 1–2 new mid-tier tech SKUs per half-year to broaden the base.
